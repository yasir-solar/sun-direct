# Sun Direct Renewable — Launch Checklist

The public interface omits unsupported business claims. Confirm the operational items below before deployment.

## Brand and business

- [ ] Confirm legal business name and public trading name
- [ ] Provide final vector logo if a client-owned mark will supersede the coded sun-and-panel identity
- [ ] Approve company story and team information before publishing biographies
- [ ] Supply licences, insurance and certifications only when approved for public use
- [ ] Confirm warranty language and responsible parties
- [ ] Confirm equipment brands the business is authorized to promote

## Contact and service

- [ ] Public phone number
- [ ] Public email address
- [ ] Office or mailing address, if it should be published
- [ ] Business hours
- [ ] Confirmed service radius and communities served
- [ ] Privacy and accessibility contact
- [ ] Verified Google Business Profile and social URLs
- [ ] Confirm ownership of `sundirectrenewable.ca` or set the final canonical domain

## Projects and proof

- [ ] Confirm publication permission for all supplied photographs and videos
- [ ] Add project locations only where customers have approved disclosure
- [ ] Add system sizes, equipment and completion dates only when verified
- [ ] Add production figures only with their calculation method
- [ ] Supply authentic testimonials with source, date and permission records

## Forms and compliance

- [ ] Configure the secure form or CRM endpoint
- [ ] Configure spam protection
- [ ] Confirm secure electricity-bill upload handling and retention/deletion rules
- [ ] Review privacy policy against actual vendors and processes
- [ ] Obtain legal review of terms and CASL consent workflow
- [ ] Add analytics only after privacy and consent requirements are addressed
